BISCUITS

Build and Simulate Circuits

Objective:

Design relevant circuits for the provided questions. The fastest solver wins!

Outline:

The competition will follow a cryptic hunt format. The participants will have to build circuits relevant to the given questions and share their links on the website. Note that subsequent levels will be unlocked only after the submission has been evaluated and marked correct.

Participants will have to use the following platforms to design the circuits:

  • TinkerCad

  • Arduino IDE

  • Falstad

The participants will be notified which circuit simulator is to be used for each level.

The questions will be based on circuit analysis, logic gates, sensors, code debugging (Arduino/C), basic computer architecture and electronic signals.
